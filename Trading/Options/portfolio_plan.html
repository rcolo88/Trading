Execution Notes

  • Roll all calls at 50% max profit (typically day 12-18 of a 30-45 DTE cycle). This extracts theta decay approximately 2x faster than holding to expiration. Exception: WMT, PG, DUK, PEP — use 14-21 DTE cycles for these lower-IV names to reduce roll frequency.
  • Earnings management: Close calls 2 trading days before earnings for AAPL, AVGO, MSFT, CAT, XOM, JPM, WMT, PG. Reopen the day after the report to capture IV crush as the premium seller. DUK, PEP, MRK, ABBV can ride through earnings.
  • Dividend capture risk: ABBV, JPM, XOM, HD, LMT have the highest payout ratios. If a call is deep ITM before the ex-dividend date, close or roll the position to avoid early assignment.
  • Downside management: In a market drawdown, you still own all 16 quality names — no margin, no forced liquidation. Premiums typically increase in down markets (VIX spikes). Dividends are safe (payout ratios all below 70%). Continue selling calls at the same delta targets; strikes simply reset lower.
  • Cash buffer: Keep ~$300 residual cash for flexibility on rolling assignments.
